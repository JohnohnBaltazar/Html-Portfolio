This is How I cook my Favorite Chiken Adobo

Ingredients

  • Chicken - 0.1kg
  • Garlic - 1 head
  • Bay Leaf - 1 Pice
  • Soy Sauce - 2 Tbls
  • Water - 1 Cup
  • Pineapple Juice - 1 Cup
  • Vegatable Oil - 1 Tbls

    • This is the Instruction or the Step by step on how I cook it

    Step 1

    Combine Chicken, garlic,Bay leaf, Soy sauce, Pineapple Juice and Water. Simmer for 15 Minutes. Remove Chicken and Set Aside.Continue to simmer for 30 mintues.

    Step 2

    Saute Chicken in oil until golden brown. Pour heat oil in a seperste pan.

    Step 3

    Pour Cooking Liquid and Simmer for Five Minutes, Transfer into a Serving Plate and Serve.
